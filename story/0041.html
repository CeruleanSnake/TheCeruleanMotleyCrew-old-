!!Warning, Author Commentary may contain spoilers!!



Animating the glasses panel was probably my fav. It was actually pretty easy, just slap on a few effects and bam!
I also needed to establish Chever's cool eye patch instead of just pulling it out of nowhere.

This is probably the first personal interaction between Chever and Jujuju. With Jujuju, I stuck with a theme of 3 as a piss take on Sollux's duality theme, plus also it matches her symbol and is an important reoccurring number throughout the comic.
Especially considering the comic started on the 3rd of November (2nd US time but that doesn't count). This page was also published on the 3rd of January (magic innit?).

It's also the first time a trans character is revealed. There's 3 in total (LOOK 3 AGAIN!!!) but we find out later. I really wanted a positive story surrounding trans characters who are actually happy as opposed to the multitude of miserable trans stories on the internet.
As a trans person myself, I personally find that to be really disappointing... so here we go! Zany adventures with trans characters!!

-TheCeruleanSnake
