!!Warning, Author Commentary may contain spoilers!!



Freya's introduction is quite similar to how it was in the original version. However, she spoke with more of a scottish accent. I decided to switch it up to something more spanish considering every troll and their lusus speaks in some form of scottish style in the comic, considering the pirate shit and all.

Her name (aside from Freya) is also a mix of Spanish words (and one fudged word to fit the F theme) relating to fire. It's also comically long strictly for the funniez, not because of anything else, really.

Why did I pick Spanish? If you remember from one of my earlier quips, it's one of the 3 settings that always shows up in pirate stories, plus has some really fun architecture to draw.

I'm not a native speaker, so I've got some help on the side to make sure things sound at least decent (thanks Fruz!)

Also, god damn I hated drawing that dress, so much lines.
