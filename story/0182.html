So, Govani was actually a spur-of-the-moment character.

I was watching a program that was talking about a highwayman in 1600s England and I ended up drawing a bit of inspiration. I gave him a spanish flair to fit the pirate feel. Plus, what better way to show how fancy he is than with fancy cursive text?

The name Govani is also a compressed version of the Italian name, Giovanni. His last name I just slapped on the spot.
