My reasons for rebooting?

I had to take a very long break due to health and I wasn't satisfied about how I was handling the story. I went back to the drawing board, sat down and re thought the story as I wish to make it even better. Now that I am much better thanks to doctors, I have the strength to make this comic even more enjoyable for you!



First Started on 3rd Nov 2020.
Born out of passion and interest to explore the space expansion of the Trolls set on ancient Alternia from Homestuck. Aiming to be mostly a comedy and capture the whimsical joke nature of early Homestuck.
